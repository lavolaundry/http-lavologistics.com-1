Why Lavo?

Quality, speed, and ease of use are our three priorities, we are always trying to deliver a great customer experience. With Lavo, you’ll no longer have to worry about doing your own laundry or even dropping off or picking it up at the laundromat. Simply tap a button on your phone and have your laundry or dry-cleaning picked up and delivered back to your home, workplace, or hotel.

What sets us apart

We founded Lavo with two goals. First, we wanted to make on-demand cleaning cheap and accessible to individuals who do not have the time to wash their own clothes. Secondly, we wanted to reform the laundry industry. Instead of complex per pound charges, different item fees, and archaic price structures, we created a custom laundry and garment bag with only two rates: half or full. Go ahead, stuff your bag to your desire or leave your laundry below the line. Life is complicated enough, your laundry shouldn’t be too.

Our Partners

Lavo does not own and operate its own laundry facilities. Instead, we’ve developed a growing network of partner organizations who work with us to fulfill our clients’ needs. In doing so, we are able to reach more areas, charge lower rates, and give back the majority of our revenues to small laundry stores across New York City. All of our locations are quality tested and must maintain both Yelp and internal reviews of 4.5 stars or higher.
